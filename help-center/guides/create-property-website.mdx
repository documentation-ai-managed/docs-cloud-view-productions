## Overview
Cloud View Productions lets you create a single-property website in minutes.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Select property">
    From your dashboard, click the property you want to feature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Create Website'">
    Hit the "Create Website" button at the top of the property page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Add photos, choose a theme, and edit details like address and realtor info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click "Publish". Your dedicated website link will be generated instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
