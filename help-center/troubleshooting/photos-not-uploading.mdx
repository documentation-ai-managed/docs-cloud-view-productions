---
title: Photos Not Uploading
description: What to do if your media upload is stuck.
---

## Symptoms
- Photo upload progress bar gets stuck
- "Upload failed" error message

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Try reloading the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="File size and format">
    Make sure each photo is under 20MB and in JPG or PNG format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try another browser">
    Attempt upload in Chrome or Firefox.
  </Step>
</Steps>
