## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A valid email address for account creation
- Property details ready (address, photos, floor plans)
- Access to a modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox

<Callout kind="info">
  No credit card required for signup. Upgrade to premium services after your first project.
</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to set up your Cloud View Productions account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://dashboard.example.com/signup` and enter your email, name, and password.

    Choose your account type: agent, broker, or photographer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Cloud View Productions.

    Click the link to activate your account. Verification takes `<30s`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://dashboard.example.com/login` and sign in with your credentials.

    Complete the quick onboarding tour.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Property Details

Provide media for processing into tours, websites, and brochures.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Files" icon="upload">
    Gather high-res photos (>2MP), floor plans (PDF), and MLS data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload via Dashboard" icon="cloud-upload">
    Drag-and-drop files in the `Property Details` section.

    Or use our API for bulk uploads:
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process Order" icon="settings">
    Review preview and approve for production.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,cURL">
  ```javascript
  const formData = new FormData();
  formData.append('photos', fileInput.files[0]);
  formData.append('propertyId', 'prop-12345');
  formData.append('apiKey', 'YOUR_API_KEY');

  await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/properties/prop-12345/media', {
    method: 'POST',
    body: formData,
  });
  ```
  ```bash
  curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/properties/prop-12345/media \
    -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
    -F "photos=@property-photos.zip" \
    -F "propertyId=prop-12345"
  ```
</CodeGroup>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Processing starts immediately. Expect a dedicated property website live in `<48h`.
</Callout>
